Hello Vincent and Alan,

 

We are happy to provide some lathe slitting advice in terms of AFT product A11T0 – transparent 2mm thick - which is not easy to slit.

 

Our machine set up and  good practice will be as follows:

·         A sharp, clean blade.

·         Grind the blade every 3-4 cuts.

·         Mandrel rotation speed: 130-140 RPM

·         Spinning blade (same direction as mandrel) 100 RPM.

·         The blade must come into each cut slowly. Too fast and it can ‘bounce’.

·         Wet slit: using a  95% water and 5% lubricant ratio.

 

We hope this is helpful.

 

Kind regards,

Martin Saville.
