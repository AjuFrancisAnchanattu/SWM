Many thanks for your phone call earlier today.

 

I will try and summarise things for you both.

 

·         SAP 144290 - 5419 Black Jumbo 1mm x 1200mm – current Adhere price £3.28 per linear metre. Margin 34 % GM on standard (current) costing.

·         SAP 149678 - 5404 White Jumbo 1mm x 1200mm WP – current Adhere price £2.87 per linear metre. Margin 29 % GM on standard (current) costing.

 

I feel sure these prices are correct.

 

If Adhere are questioning why the 5419 is more expensive than the 5404 – well, a price agreed by Dawn and because 5419 is a different product to 5404!

 

Differences: the 5419 comes with a different grade of PE (polyethylene) foam to 5404; the adhesive for both is acrylic but a different grade. The paper is white glassine paper – the same for both.

 

If Adhere want 5409 – “black coloured 5404”then you would have to ask them:

 

·         Do you want 5409 on a white paper (the standard 5409 paper is amber coloured)?

·         Adhere would have to accept an MOQ of 1,260 linear meters (3 jumbos) if white paper was necessary. (I have checked this with production)

·         Pricing: well, dry black foam is more expensive than white…Dawn clearly you would need to somehow bridge the gap on the pricing, ensuring your 5419 margin level is retained.

 

Scapa would have to create a new SAP part number using the IJF system if Adhere choose to buy 5409 black 1mm thick jumbo.

 

Hope this helps.
