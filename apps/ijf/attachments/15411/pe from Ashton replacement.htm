The stock of standard 174 with filmic liner, stocked in Ashton has been sold out and will not be replenished again. For Europe we have developed a version with paper liner, which we continue calling 174 and is stocked in Valence (due to width constraint of Jumbo Ashton could not handle).

 

This new product with paper liner is a bit more expensive compared to the old version, new pricing guidelines are attached.

Please note that in these attached guidelines the PL base price is the end user price and level 2 prices are the supposed to be prices to be offered to our preferred distributors.

 

We decided to change to a paper liner as we had big potentials found but the version with filmic liner was a barrier to explore these new potentials.

 

174 seems to be a very good product for temporary fixing. High adhesion and good removability. It can really compete with Tesa 4964 and at those customers that already tested 174

liked our product more.  Because of its thickness it can also very well be used for plate mounting applications.
